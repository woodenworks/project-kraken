## Unlucky Coin Purse
*Wondrous item, very rare*

This coin purse is made of unusual leather.

This coin contains 666 silver pieces. No matter how many coins you add or withdraw, or what metal those coins may be, every day at dusk it resets to 666 silver pieces.

***First Curse.*** This coin purse draws the eyes of criminals. Whenever you are in a shady part of town, the GM should roll to determine whether a robbery attempt is made.
